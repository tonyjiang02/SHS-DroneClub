Drone and Aviation Club

Who Are We?

We are the Saratoga High School Drone/Aviation club. Dedicated to the engineering, design, and programming of drones and aviation machines.

Officer Team:

What Do We Do?

Our mission is to inspire the student community to gain insight on the aeronautical engineering industry as well as provide members with a practical understanding of the myriad of components that go into building a drone or plane.

We will be building inexpensive drones througout the course of the year with build meetings on the weekends and meetups during lunch.
